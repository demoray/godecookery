A dish of raisins & rice - contributed by L. J. Spencer, Jr.
  • 1 cp. Rice
  • 2 cps Water 
  • A handful of Zante raisins
  • tsp. salt
Bring water to a boil. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil again. Reduce heat to the lowest possible setting. Cover tightly. Cook for 20 mins. Remove from heat. Leave for 5 mins. 

Serves 6.
