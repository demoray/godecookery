XXV. Olive Oil (Oleum Oliue)

Nature: Warm and humid.
Optimum: Good month.
Usefulness: It fattens and is easily digestible.
Dangers: It weakens the mechanism of digestion.
Neutralization of the Dangers: Mixed with foods.

From the Tacuinum of Paris
