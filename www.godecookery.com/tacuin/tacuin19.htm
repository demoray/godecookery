XIX. Sweet Marjoram (Maiorana)

Nature: Warm and dry in the third degree.
Optimum: The very small and aromatic variety.
Usefulness: It is good for a cold and humid stomach.
Dangers: None.
Effects: It purifies the blood. It is good for cold and humid teperaments, for old people, in Winter, in Autumn, and in cold regions.

From the Tacuinum of Vienna
