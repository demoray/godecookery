XLV. Spring (Ver)

Nature: Moderate humidity in the second degree.
Optimum: Its central period.
Usefulness: Good for all animals and for the products that germinate from the earth.
Dangers: Bad for unclean bodies.
Neutralization of the Dangers: By cleaning the body.

From the Tacuinum of Paris
