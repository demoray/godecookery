XIV. Wheat (Fermentum)

Nature: Warm and humid in the second degree.
Optimum: Plump and heavy grains.
Usefulness: Opens abcesses.
Dangers: Causes obstructions.
Neutralization of the Dangers: When it is well prepared.

From the Theatrum of Casanatense
