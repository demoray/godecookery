XVI. Sour Pomegranates (Granata Acetosa)

Nature: Cold in the second degree and humid in the first.
Optimum: Those that are very watery.
Usefulness: Good for a warm liver.
Dangers: Bad for the chest and the voice.
Neutralization of the Dangers: With honeyed foods.

From the Tacuinum of Rouen
