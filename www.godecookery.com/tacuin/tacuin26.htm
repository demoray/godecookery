XXVI. Partridges (Perdices)

Nature: Of moderate warmth.
Optimum: Those that are moist and fat.
Usefulness: Good for convalescing people.
Dangers: Bad for those engaged in heavy labor.
Neutralization of the Dangers: Cooking them with leavened dough.

From the Theatrum of Casanatense
