XXXI. Ricotta (Recocta)

Nature: Cold and humid.
Optimum: That obtained from pure milk.
Usefulness: It nourishes the body and fattens it.
Dangers: It causes occlusions, is difficult to digest, and favors colic.

From the Tacuinum of Paris
