• Ro, Roes The Roe deer (Capreolus capreolus). A European variety of small deer, reddish-brown in the summer and grey in the winter, noted for its nimbleness and grace.

    • "The dredful ro, the buk, the hert and the hynde" - The Parlement of Foules

    "Dredful" means "timid."

    "Was ful the woode, and many roes" - The Book of the Duchess
