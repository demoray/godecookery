  • Notemuge Nutmeg. One of the most common of medieval spices, used as extensively then as it is today. It was featured in recipes ranging from meats to beverages, and must have been found in nearly every kitchen. It was quite often used in combination with such spices as cinnamon, cloves, galingale, and grains of paradise.

    • "And notemuge to putte in ale" - Sir Thopas
