What Happened to The Pestilence Tyme?

After 7 years on the Internet, it's time for The Pestilence Tyme to say good-bye.

Gode Cookery is in the process of refining itself into a domain concentrating specifically on medieval & Renaissance food & cookery; sadly, we are saying good-bye to both Jeanne La pucelle & The Pestilence Tyme on August 25th, 2004, but are happily looking forward to expanding in many other ways.

We thank you for your support of The Pestilence Tyme and Gode Cookery.
