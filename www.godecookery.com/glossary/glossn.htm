N
  • Namely - especially.
  • Nauell - navel.
  • Nesh; Nessh; Nesshe; Neysshe - soft.
  • Neþer - nether, lower.
  • Noght; Not - not.
  • Notemygge - nutmegs.
  • Noteye - nutty.
  • Noþer - nor.
  • Notys - nuts.
  • Nygh - near; nigh.
  • Nyt - night(s).
  • Nym(e) - take.
